The importance of daily exercise

A lot of people involve in daily physical activiuties,to maintain a healthy body and mind.
Studies show that regular exercise helps reduce anxiety,negative mood.etc

This are some of a few benefit of regular exercise

1.Improves physical health.

Regular exercise strenthens the hearth, improves circulation and maintain blood pressure.

2.Enhance mental health.

Regular movement releases chemical in the brain,that reduces stress,anxiety and depression.
